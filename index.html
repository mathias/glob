blip rot prototype puzzle page

Hi there! This is a very early prototype for puzzles in the game blip_rot.

blip_rot is a game of adventure, set in the world of Arc. Something has gone terribly wrong in Arc, tearing apart the world itself. You embark on a quest to find out what happened and ultimately, fix the world. Throughout the journey, you'll be accompanied by a little flying robot, seen at the right. The robot will help you through the various challenges by providing information and history. Your companion won't be fully functional for *this* prototype, but will try to give you some hints.

The main type of puzzles I'm working on use a visual programming language to solve simple coding problems. I've named this programming language glob. You'll be able to do lots with glob's blocks in the game, but mostly you will use it to solve puzzles.

Your feedback will help determine how the puzzles appear in the game. At the end of the puzzles, I have a link to a survey, and I would really appreciate it if you filled it out. Thanks and have fun! — @mathiasx
